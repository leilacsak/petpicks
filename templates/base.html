PetPicks Guidelines
Welcome to PetPicks!

To ensure a fair and fun experience for everyone, please follow these guidelines when participating:

Entry Requirements
  • Submit a clear, recent photo of your pet
  • Provide accurate information about your pet (name, breed, age)
  • Each pet can only be entered once per round
  • Your pet must be a real, living animal
Photo Guidelines
  • Photo must clearly show your pet
  • No filters or heavy editing allowed
  • Photos can be JPG, PNG, or WEBP format
  • Maximum file size: 5 MB
Community Standards
  • Be respectful and kind in comments
  • No spam or promotional content
  • Celebrate all pets equally
  • Follow all applicable laws and regulations
Rewards

At this stage, PetPicks does not offer monetary rewards. Winners receive digital badges and recognition in our community.

Moderation

All entries are reviewed by our moderation team before being approved. Entries that violate these guidelines may be rejected.
