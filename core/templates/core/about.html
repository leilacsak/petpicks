About PetPicks

PetPicks is a community engagement platform designed for pet owners who enjoy fun and friendly competitions. Users can upload a photo of their pet and enter active lottery rounds for a chance to win digital achievement badges.

Each lottery round is managed by an administrator to ensure fairness and transparency. Only approved entries are included in the draw, and winners are selected randomly.

PetPicks focuses on creativity, community participation, and positive engagement. No monetary rewards are involved - winners receive digital badges as recognition.

Have fun!

Get Started in 3 Steps

  • 1 Upload a photo
  • 2 Enter a Draw
  • 3 Win a Badge

How It Works

  1. Create an account and log in to PetPicks
  2. Browse active lottery rounds and find one you like
  3. Upload a photo of your pet and fill in the entry details
  4. Submit your entry before the round deadline
  5. Wait for the administrator to review and approve your entry
  6. Once approved, your pet is included in the random draw
  7. Check back to see if you've won a digital badge
  8. Display your badges on your profile and celebrate with the community
